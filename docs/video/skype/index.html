Skype

Official website

Skype is one of the original telecommunication platforms for video chat. It was acquired my Microsoft and is geared towards users within their ecosystem and for ease-of-use.

Ease of use

Like, Zoom or other major video-conferencing platforms, Skype is very user friendly and easy to set up. However, for users who want more customization, there is little one can to do for a more high fidelity connection.


caution

There have been many known security/privacy issues associated with Skype over the years. Only recently have they supported E2E encryption, but this claim cannot be validated. Thus, we cannot recommended this service.
