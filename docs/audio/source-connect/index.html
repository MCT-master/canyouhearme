Source-Connect is a service from Source-Elements, currently in beta, that provides low-latency audio connections with up to 8 people over WebRTC.
  • Overview
  • Specifications

Basic information

Developer: Source Elements

Website: https://now.source-elements.com/

Protocol: Audio over WebRTC

OS Supported: Operates on a Chromium based browser

Pricing: Free (up to 4 people per session)

Requires sign-up?: Yes for hosting, no for joining

Detailed information

Features:

Self-hosted?: No

Recommended net-speed: 1 Mbps

Required net-speed: Unknown

Max quality: Stereo, 512kbps

Latency (estimate): 10ms

Last update: Unknown version - online service

Open source?: No

Ease of use

Simply sign in and connect, you can invite guests with a url, as a Guest all you have to do is connect. The audio quality can be configured (stereo/mono as well) for better latency and less digital distortion. The service can host sessions of up to eight people.


note

If members of a session refresh to update settings, they often have to wait until the other members refresh their page as well to hear/see them. This makes large group calls quite difficult and incredibly unstable.

caution

This service is in beta! Use on an experimental basis.
