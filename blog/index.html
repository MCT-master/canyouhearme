An Example Guide

April 28, 2020

Jackson Goode

MCT

Using the tab functionality we can easily allow users to switch between instructions based on their operating system.

  • macOS
  • Windows

Here is an example of what a detailed guide might look like for Zoom on macOS. 👀

  1. Download Zoom
    1. Visit zoom.us/download
    2. Download macOS version of Zoom
    3. Click through installation
    4. Open Zoom by searching Spotlight
  2. To connect with Zoom
    1. Accept “Unknown developer” warning during opening
    2. Enter the number of the Zoom room you’re connecting to
  3. To host with Zoom
    1. Sign in or register a new account
      1. Click top right profile icon
      2. Fill our registration
      3. Verify email
    2. Click, “New Meeting”
    3. Click Invite (multiple options below)
      1. Send invitees the Zoom Meeting ID to connect
      2. Search through list for contacts
      3. Click “Copy Invitation” to and then paste in message/email to invites
      4. Click “Email” and use email service to send invitation
  4. Optimizations
    1. Under “Settings: Video”, click “Enable HD video”
      1. Offers better image quality from your video source
    2. Under “Settings: Audio: Advanced”, click “Enable Original Sound toggle”
      1. Disables Zoom audio processing, for raw audio quality
      2. On main Zoom video window click “Enable Original Sound”
